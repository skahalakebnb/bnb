Our Prices

At Skaha LakeFront BnB, we offer a selection of charming and comfortable suites designed to suit every type of traveler. Our rates are per night and include full access to our stunning amenities, breathtaking lake views, and the tranquility of our lakeside retreat. Each of our suites is located on the main floor and comfortably accommodates 2 adults, with plenty of space for children to join in the relaxation.

    Our Lakeview Queen Suite is a cozy, inviting space with a queen-size bed, offering a peaceful haven for couples or solo travelers. Wake up to beautiful lake views and enjoy the comforts of home in this stylish suite. This room accommodates 2 adults and children.
  • The Skaha Suite (Double Bed): $199/night
    Ideal for solo guests or couples, The Skaha Suite features a comfortable double bed and provides a charming, homely atmosphere. Relax and unwind in this welcoming space, which is perfect for those seeking a peaceful retreat. This suite accommodates 2 adults and children.

    For those looking for ultimate privacy and luxury, book the entire main floor of our retreat. This exclusive package includes all three suites, along with access to the spacious living room, dining room, covered deck, and hot tub. Perfect for families, groups, or anyone looking for a complete, private getaway with all the comforts of home. This entire floor comfortably accommodates up to 6 adults and children.

Things to Do

Located in the heart of the Okanagan Valley, Skaha LakeFront BnB offers a perfect base to explore the stunning natural beauty and diverse activities of the area.

  • Outdoor Recreation: Enjoy swimming, kayaking, and paddleboarding on Lake Skaha, or explore the Kettle Valley Rail Trail and Skaha Bluffs for hiking and mountain biking.
  • Wine & Craft Beer: Experience the local wine tours and visit Penticton’s renowned craft breweries for a taste of the region’s finest beverages.
  • Family Fun: Relax on nearby sandy beaches, float along the Okanagan Channel, or explore the Penticton Farmers Market for fresh local produce and handmade goods.
  • Winter Activities (Seasonal): Ski, snowboard, or snowshoe at Apex Mountain Resort, just a short drive away.

Whether you’re seeking adventure or relaxation, Skaha LakeFront BnB is the ideal starting point to enjoy all that Penticton has to offer.
